Welcome to the Kaomoji / Japanese Smiley Customizer! This web page allows you to create and customizer fun, Japanese-style smileys called kaomoji. Kaomoji are made of various UTF-8 characters — so their appearance can differ depending on: the client who is viewing them, the character set of the website you're posting them on, and way the browse renders them. For this reason, I reccomend that you check the small preview on the bottom-left-hand of this web page to get a feel for how your smiley will look when in its small form. Once you're happy with your kaomoji, click the Copy to Clipboard button and share it with your friends!
